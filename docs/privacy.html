Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 27, 2025

Your privacy is important. This Privacy Policy explains how Glow Bar handles user information.

01

Information We Collect

Glow Bar does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

Specifically:

  • No account creation
  • No personal identifiers
  • No analytics
  • No tracking
  • No third-party SDKs that collect user data
02

Device Permissions

Glow Bar may access system features such as audio output or haptic feedback solely to provide the interactive experience. These features are used locally on the device and no data is transmitted or recorded.

03

Data Sharing

Glow Bar does not share data with third parties.

04

Children's Privacy

Glow Bar does not knowingly collect any information from children. Since no data collection occurs, no personal information is stored.

05

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated occasionally. Any changes will be reflected with an updated date.
