Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 26, 2025

Phone Vape is a novelty application created for entertainment purposes only.

Data Collection

Phone Vape does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

Microphone Access

Phone Vape may request access to the device microphone solely to detect breath sounds for triggering visual and haptic animations. All audio is processed entirely on-device and is never recorded, stored, or transmitted.

Device Sensors

Phone Vape may use device motion sensors or system volume changes to enable interactive effects. This information is used only in real time and is not stored or shared.

Third Parties

Phone Vape does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, tracking technologies, or external services.

Children

Phone Vape is not intended for children and does not knowingly collect data from anyone under the age of 13.

Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
